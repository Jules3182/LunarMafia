About us

What we do

After meeting in our home town, Wethersfield CT, mangakapdat, Blanco, and I realized we had the same vision and drive. We began working on clothing, music, and video production to spread our brand across all areas of our industry.

We strive to become a musical dynasty within the industry, and spread our ideas and vision to the community. This website is the home of our ideas, services, and overall baseline of Lunar Media Productions. We look to break through the glass ceiling surrounding the musical and fashion industry by delivering innovative and outside of the box media to our fanbase.
